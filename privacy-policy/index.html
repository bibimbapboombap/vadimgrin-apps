Privacy Policy — Just Chess Clock

Last updated: 30 June 2026

Just Chess Clock (“the app”) is a chess clock for iPhone. This policy explains how the app handles your information. The short version: the app does not collect, store, or share any personal data.

Information we collect

None. The app does not ask you to create an account or provide a name, email address, or any other personal information. There is no sign-up and no login.

The app does not:

  • collect or transmit personal data;
  • use analytics, advertising, or tracking of any kind;
  • include third-party SDKs or services;
  • access your camera, microphone, photos, contacts, location, calendar, or health data;
  • send anything over the network — the app contains no networking code and works entirely offline.

Data stored on your device

The app saves a single setting on your device: the time control (time preset) you last selected, so it can be restored the next time you open the app. It is stored using Apple’s standard on-device preferences, and it:

  • never leaves your device;
  • is not visible or accessible to us;
  • is permanently removed when you delete the app.

Everything else — the running clocks, move counts, and game results — exists only while the app is open. It is never saved or transmitted.

Third parties

The app contains no third-party SDKs, advertising networks, or analytics services. We do not sell, rent, trade, or share any information, because we do not collect any.

Your rights

Because the app collects no personal data, there is nothing for us to access, correct, export, or delete on your behalf. You remain in full control: deleting the app removes the one setting it stores.

Information Apple may collect

When you download the app from the App Store, Apple may collect information such as download and crash statistics under its own policies. If you have chosen to share analytics with developers, we may see aggregated, anonymised diagnostics through Apple’s tools. That information does not identify you personally and is governed by Apple’s privacy policy, not this one.

Children’s privacy

The app is suitable for all ages. It does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under 13, because it does not collect information from anyone at all.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new “Last updated” date.

Contact

If you have any questions about this policy, contact me.
